## Getting Help

- **Questions & Discussions**: [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/avalonreset/seo-dungeon/discussions)

## Before Opening an Issue

1. Search [existing issues](https://github.com/avalonreset/seo-dungeon/issues) for duplicates
2. Include your OS, Node.js version, and the full error output
